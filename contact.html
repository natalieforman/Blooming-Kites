Directions

Parking

  • Please park at Hains Point

  • Take Rock Creek and Potomac Pkwy NW to Ohio Dr SW.

  • Continue onto Independence Ave SW and The lot will be on your right.

  • A shuttle will run between Hains Point and the Tidal Basin.

Hains Point

Metro

  • Take the Orange or Blue metro lines.

  • The closest metro station to the Washington Monument is the Smithsonian metro station

  • Head north towards Jefferson Dr. SW

  • Turn left onto Jefferson Dr. SW

WMATA

Biking

  • Capital Bikeshare will be operating bike share stations.

  • The closest bike rack is along Jefferson Dr. SW between 14th and 15th Sreets SW.

  • There will be extra bike racks at Independence Avenue & 15th St., SW,.
